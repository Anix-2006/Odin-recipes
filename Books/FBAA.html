Blood And Ash

Author: Jennifer L. Armentrout

A sweeping saga of passion, prophecy, and peril.

The "Blood and Ash" series, written by Jennifer L. Armentrout, is a fantasy romance series set in a world where the Ascended, a group of god-like beings, rule over mortals. The series follows Poppy, a Maiden chosen by the gods, who is destined for a ritual called Ascension. However, she yearns for freedom and begins to question the established order and the true nature of her world. The series explores themes of power, choice, and rebirth, with a focus on forbidden love, intrigue, and unexpected plot twists.
